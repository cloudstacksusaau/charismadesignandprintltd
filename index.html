Charisma Design & Print Ltd.

THE LEADING COMMERCIAL PRINTER & PRINT SHOP IN TYSELEY and GREATER BIRMINGHAM

Established in 1992 Charisma Design and Print is a leading Graphic Design and Print Shop based in Tyseley, Birmingham, West Midlands.

With over 30 years of experience, we are one of the most successful litho and digital printers in the greater Birmingham area, using the most up-to-date machinery and technology to successfully deliver on all client projects. Our team of in house, fully trained graphic designers or sales team are here to discuss your ideas and turn them into highly effective, market led designs. Whatever your needs, we can advise you on the best approach to get your message out there.

We offer exceptional customer service and go that extra mile to ensure you are always satisfied. Our aim is for Operational Excellence and we endeavour to produce the best commercial printer job possible.

As one of the best equipped commercial printers in the area we are able to produce a huge range of high quality products. Naturally most printed solutions are of a bespoke nature and are printed to order, however we also provide a number of pre-priced and pre-sized items, as well as stocked items.

Charisma Design & Print invests heavily in the latest printing and finishing technology to give our customers the very best in colour accuracy and the highest quality print available. Printing is carried out on the latest, fully automated Heidelberg presses, ensuring your job is printed quickly and efficiently.

Using the latest computer to plate (CTP) technology, high quality single, multiple spot colour and process colour work is achieved by our fully skilled Charisma print operatives.

We also provide a digital print service that is second to none. We have invested in a digital print solution that utilises the Konica Minolta press giving results that equal more traditional litho print.

If you are looking for a reliable, dedicated and exceptional commercial printing service with creative design direction, high-quality printing and a deluxe range of finishes at competitive prices, look no further than Charisma Design & Print.
